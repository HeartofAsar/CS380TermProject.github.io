About Us

PhinStore was created with students in mind. We know how hetic campus life can be, so we built a space where JU students can easily find everything they need. From school supplies and tech essentials to camps merch that shows off school pride.

Our Beginnings

PhinStore began as a web programming project with the goal of creating a practical and user-friendly online store for Jacksonville University students. Recognizing the usefulness of a centralized platform where students could easily access campus merchandise, school supplies, and tech essentials, this project was designed to combine functionality with convenience.


Our mission

Our mission is to simplify student life by providing a one-stop shop for essential items while delivering a smooth and user-friendly online experience.
