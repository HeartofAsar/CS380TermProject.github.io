Frequently Asked Questions

At PhinStore, we strive to assist JU students by answering common questions and providing helpful information about our products and services. Whether you're shopping for campus merch, school supplies, or tech, we're here to make your experience smooth and easy.

Can I cancel or change my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified or canceled within a limited time after being placed. Please contact support or vist.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major debit/credit cards and flex payment.

How long does it take to receive my order?

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days. Delievery or pickup times may vary depending on availability.

Are products officially affiliated with campus?

Yes, all merchandise is approved for campus use.

What is your return policy?

Items can be returned within a specified period (7-14 days) if they are unused and in original condition

What if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?

Please contact support immediately with your order details. We will arrange a replacement or refund

Can I use flex to pay?

Yes! Students may use their flex card as a payment method.
